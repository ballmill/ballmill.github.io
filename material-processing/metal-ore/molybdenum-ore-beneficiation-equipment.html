    • Host: Molybdenum is found relatively late. But it has the advantages of high strength, high melting point, corrosion resistance, wear resistance research and other advantages, so it has been widely used in industry. Today, we invite the expert—Mr. Wang to introduce the molybdenum ore application and its beneficiation equipment. Welcome!
    • Mr. Wang: Hi. Good morning. It's my honor to come here. Hope you have a great morning!
    • Host: Mr. Wang, as far as I know, although molybdenum's found is late, it plays an important role in industry, doesn't it?
    • Mr. Wang: Yes. In the metallurgical industry, molybdenum is often as the additive in producing all kinds of alloy steel. Molybdenum alloy steel is used to make transportation device, locomotive, industrial machinery, and various kinds of instrument. Alloy of molybdenum and tungsten, chromium or vanadium is used to manufacture the alloy components and parts for warships, tanks, guns, rocket, and satellite. In the chemical industry, molybdenum is mainly used for lubricant, catalyst and pigment. Molybdenum chemical products are widely used in dye, ink, color precipitate dye, and anticorrosive primer.

Of course, you can also use the high-frequency screen to choose the suitable size. All the beneficiation machines is provided. You can select the best suitable machines.

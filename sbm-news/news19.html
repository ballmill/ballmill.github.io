Moving forward together: Shibang holds the 2014 oath-taking rally

On February 7, 2013, 8th of the first month by the Lunar Calendar, Shibang Group's 2014 oath-taking rally was held on the East China Sea coast for the first time. In face of the ordeals, Shibang Group's chairman, members of the Board of Directors and the Administration Committee and the employees make the strongest voice at the beginning of 2014 in the grand and beautiful scene.

We work together with one heart, managing to take the lead in 2014

In the heroic song of "Laughing over the blue sea", the flags and Tai Chi performance that contains the beauty and strength of Chinese culture unveil Shibang's 2014 oath-taking rally. "Serving the customer, doing an even better job, this is our common goal and responsibility!" "In the new year, we will focus on product positioning, do the job in a more focused, professional, refined and promoted way! The large-scale production system will flourish through efforts! " "In 2014, the functional departments are committed to: strategic guidance, promotion of culture, well-defined process, attentive service; risk management and control, resource rationing, value creation and share of happiness." Heads of marketing, production, and functional departments take to the stage and lead their people to shout out the announcements and goals of the departments: to have an apartment immediately, to have a quality taste immediately, to become literate immediately...... These announcements are not only the expectations of the departments for 2014, but also their commitments to the customers.

Youth, vitality, passion, and dream, we are to advance side by side in 2014

Then, 60 Shibang employees sent New Year greetings to everyone with the horse riding dance, setting off a climax of the meeting again. The vibrant dance is not only related to the lunar year of the horse, but also fits the theme of the 2014 Shibang oath-taking rally - "twenty fourteen, advancing side by side in 2014.

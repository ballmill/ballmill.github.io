18 Types of Star Machines to Launch at SBM Industrial Forest Recreate buama China Legend at E6.120

The sector of machinery entered a relatively stable increasing stage at a moderate speed since 2013, although it went through the fluctuation in the past years. There was some fluctuation to the operation of macro-economy and adverse factors exist in the sector, such as the difficulty in collecting the payment, increasing financing cost and instable orders in the first half of this year, but the overall performance in the sector of machinery was in a stable-and-progress operation status, increasingly companies were focusing on the R&D on competitive high-end products and extension services, and the promoted internal power which facilitated the structure adjustment and upgrading. From November 25th to 28th, 2014, the powerful return of the 7th International Trade Fair for Construction Machinery, Building Material Machines, Construction Vehicles and Equipment (bauma China 2014), the largest industry trade fair in Asia-pacific region, will inject new power for the entire industry. Moreover, SBM, an old friend of bauma which have created several miracles, will launch the star machines, surprising discounts and brand activity to the global customers to exhibit the glamour of marked enterprise in the sector of machinery equipment.

The first exposition for construction machinery in Asia

SBM to Make Efforts to complete the Trade Fair Bauma China, held every two years, has been closely linking the development track of China construction machinery since 2002, and become the top largest dedicated trade fair in Asia-pacific region. It integrates the most competitive construction machinery brands home and abroad. Same as the exposition in the headquarters, Munich bauma in German, bauma China has been achieving constant breakthroughs regarding scope, quality and volume of business and exhibits the excellent impact as the top trade fair in the industry. It is reported bauma China 2014 covers the four categories, i.e. construction machinery, exploitation of raw materials and processing equipment, building materials machines, assorted components and service provider. Over 3,000 exhibitors from over 40 countries in the world will gather at the extra exhibition center with the area of 300,000 square meters, and it is estimated over 180,000 attendees will attend the fair.

The Flagship Exhibition Booth Displays the Resolution to be No. 1 in the World

Attendees will find the largest SBM flagship exhibition booth with the area of 775 ㎡ at Pavilion E6 No. 120. The launch of 18 types of core machines creates SBM an industrial forest. The overall design style of SBM exhibition booth takes the modern industry as the basis, and the main structure uses exposed I-beams. It not only follows the simplicity and fashion, also reflects SBM's international design and top construction requirements in the headquarters in Huadong Road, Qidong Base and Lingang Base. It exhibits SBM's resolution on striving for a world-class company with the development of hundreds of years. There are two floors at the exhibition booth. The Business Discussion Zone and Static Exhibition Zone by Models are at the upstairs, and the Product Exhibition Zone, Ore Exhibition Zone, Brand Activity Zone and Customer Photographing Zone, etc are at the first floor. It can meet the customer's demands on various visiting and discussion. In addition, a scientifically-vivid transformer with the height of 3m and model of SBM Ore Museum will launch at the exhibition booth, which will present a vivid vision combining the technology and production for attendees.

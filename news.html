Bauma China 2014 SBM Machines to Pour the Heart into Exposition and Create a Future

The 7th International Trade Fair for Construction Machinery, Building Material Machines, Construction Vehicles and Equipment (bauma China 2104) is to be held in Shanghai New International Expo Center from November 25th to 28th, 2014. Bauma China has been closely considering the development demands of markets in China and Asia since it held the 1st Exhibition in 2002. Shanghai SBM will exhibit 18 types of machines at the exposition, pour the heart into it and create a future by continuing relying on the best communication platform in the sector of project construction.

Bauma China 2014-Highest International Professional Exposition

As what was noted by Mr. Sun Xitian, president of China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Machinery Branch, that bauma China has experienced a glorious history for ten years, which fully shows the rapid rise in the sector of construction machinery in China. It boasts a successful example on exposition jointly held by China and German. It is reported the exposition area of bauma China 2014 reaches 300,000 square meters, and nearly 3,000 exhibitors from around 40 countries and regions are going to attend the exposition. It is estimated nearly 200,000 professional persons from 150 countries and regions will present and discuss at the exposition. It is believed bauma China2014 Exposition is bound to maintain the miracle of 1st Exposition and create the best exhibition and cooperation platform for exhibitors and buyers as well.

E6.120 Sparkles of Shanghai SBM with area of 755㎡ is awaiting you

The exhibition area of SBM is 775㎡, being the biggest flagship exhibition hall in bauma China E6 Pavilion at this Exposition. SBM bauma Exposition Preparatory Committee has been striving to prepare details on exposition attending since the beginning of this year, covering from constant research and development, innovation and upgrading to the launch of 18 types of real machines, from the style of Industrial Forest Exhibition Pavilion to the appearance of 3D transformers, and from the appearance of embryonic form of Ore Museum to the focus of various medias home and abroad. More various preferential policies show the emphasis and confidence of SBM on bauma China 2014.
In regard of the bauma China 2014 to be held shortly, the superintendent of SBM Team who is engaged in the preparation initiatively noted that bauma China 2014 is absolutely a very crucial platform in the international progress of SBM. The platform is not just a venue pure for trade, rather an effective platform where we make full communication with the world and customers can hear the strongest competencies of enterprises in China in deed.

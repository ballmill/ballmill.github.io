    Trapezium Mill Maintenance Skills, How To Increase Mill'S Use Life Trapezium Mill Maintenance Skills
    • Host: The trapezium mill is the domestic advanced grinding equipment. Its application is very wide. But for the machines, the maintenance is very important. It refers to the service life of the machine, and the user's economic benefits. So in order to high-efficiency production, today we invent the grinder expert--Mr. Wang to take part in the interview. Welcome to Mr. Wang.
    • Mr. Wang: Good afternoon, everyone! I'm glad to come here. Hope it is helpful for everyone.
    • Host: We know that the good maintenance is the key for the long use life. It also means the more economic benefits. So what can we do?
    • Mr. Wang: The good maintenance is simple, but it is also complicated. The preparation before start can't be ignored. Cheek whether the parts of grinder are normal. Check whether each part of bolts is tight. Ensure the working pressure of the cylinder is stable, which is to maintain a certain amount of load. Note the rolling distance adjustment. According to the process requirements, staff rotates the handwheel to adjust the rolling distance, and then lock it. You must do well the whole preparation work.
    • Host: In the production process, what need we pay attention to?
    • Mr. Wang: First, we should pay attention to the adjustment of feed mechanism. According to the size of the flow, the staff adjusts the position of limit screw, to ensure that the mill's work flow is relatively stable. Feeding should be uniform. Pay attention to the temperature of the material is rising or not. Note the suction machine adjustment, timely to reduce the material temperature. The staff should also cheek the parts lubrication situation. Clean timely the grinding roller surface. Use the brush to clean the residue on the roller surface. If find some abnormal condition, you should stop to cheek it right now.
    • Host: These are all need to be noted. If according to these points, can we increase the service life?
    • Mr. Wang: These points are only a part. In the use process, you should strictly accord with the instructions to operate. The maintenance must be regular. The work must be completed perfectly.
    • Host: Thanks Mr. Wang to tell us the maintenance skills of trapezium mill. Look forward to your next coming.
    • Mr. Wang: You are welcome. Hope to have the opportunity.

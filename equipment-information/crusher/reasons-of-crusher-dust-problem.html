    Reasons Of Crusher Dust Problem In The Stone Processing Line Reasons Of Crusher Dust Problem

    Description: In the production line of stone processing, it will produce a lot of dust. These dusts not only pollute the environment, but also seriously harm people's health. So how to solve the dust problem is very necessary. If we know the reasons of crusher dust problem, then, we can find the solutions to solve this problem in the stone processing line. Now, we will discuss the reasons. Hope it will help you to solve your problems.

    One: The filter area selection of bag type dust collector is too small. The actual amount of ventilation is insufficient. The dust exhaust hood in crusher's discharge port and the crusher cavity are difficult to form a micro negative pressure, resulting in larger of dust in the environment.

    Two: When the crushing material has a bigger humidity, after the dust particles entering into the dust collector, it will be coarse. Bag type dust collector is easy to paste bag. Discharger is easy to be blocked. This will lead to that bag type dust remover's ventilation resistance is increased significantly, and ventilation capacity is decreased. Then there will have the serious dust.

    Three: Dust enters into the dust collector. The elbow in entrance pipe will increase the resistance of the system. Elbow part is easy to wear and clog dust.

    Four: In the fall process of material on the belt, the air and the material together enter into the closed dust hood. The gas containing dust goes through the gap and orifice on the shield wall and spread to outside the hood. Then pollute the environment.

    Five: In order to reduce the air and dust leakage caused by the broken shell, in the actual production, if the material is very dry, it often sprinkles water at the entrance of the crushing machine. The result is to aggravate the pasting bag of bag type dust collector and a feeder jam.

    Six: There are more troubles in the bag type dust remover and accessories. It not only influences the dust removal, but also increases the workload of maintenance equipment. From the statistics of equipment maintenance, the poor quality parts are the important cause of equipment fault.

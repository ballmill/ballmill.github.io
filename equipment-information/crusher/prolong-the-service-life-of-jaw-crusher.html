    An Interview On Methods To Prolong The Service Life Of Jaw Crusher Prolong The Service Life Of Jaw Crusher

    Description: Any machine can be worn in use. Jaw crusher is no exception. In the daily use, jaw crusher wear is inevitable. Reducing the abrasion not only can improve the production efficiency, but also can improve the economic benefit. So what methods can prolong the service life of jaw crusher? Now, we will together discuss this issue. The following is detailed interview with Mr. Wang. Hope it will help you to improve your production efficiency.

    • Host: Welcome you coming, Mr. Wang. I believe that many audiences want to know how to prolong the service life of jaw crusher. Can you tell us some methods?
    • Mr. Wang: Yes. I'm glad. First, we must know its work principle. In the working process, the eccentric shaft is the important part. It plays the role of connecting link. In addition, the jaw plate is directly contact with the material. So it is the worst wear parts. If we can pay attention to these parts, by adopting the reasonable maintenance method, then, I believe it will increase jaw crusher's service life.
    • Host: These parts are indeed very vital. So what are the detailed maintenance methods?
    • Mr. Wang: For them, the good making material is the key factor. If the shaft wear is little, it can be cut to repair its geometry shape in the lathe, then, decrease the bearing inner diameter. If the eccentric shaft, bearing and spindle are worn, the top gap is 1.5 times than the original gap. Then, it needs to add to adjust or replace. The gap between connecting head rod and the eccentric shaft is 1.5 times larger than the assembly gap. It also needs to adjust or replace.
    • Host: So how about the maintenance of jaw plate?
    • Mr. Wang: In the production process, jaw plate and material have the direct crushing effect. The crushing force is great. It will cause the bolt and nut loosening and vibration. Encounter this kind of situation, just tighten the bolts and nuts are not completely solve the problem. We should according to the actual situation, adopt the feasible methods to solve the problem. We can use a spring anti-loose damping device to solve the loosing phenomenon in work, thereby prolong the service life of the broken plate, and improve work efficiency.
    • Host: This is a wonderful interview. Thanks Mr. Wang for your methods to prolong the service life of jaw crusher. Thank you.

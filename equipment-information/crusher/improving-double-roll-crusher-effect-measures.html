    An Introduction On Measures To Improve Double Roll Crusher Effect Improving Double Roll Crusher Effect Measures
    • Host: Improving the crushing effect is our production goal. So for this problem we are concerned, we invite the expert—Mr. Wang to give us a meaningful class. Welcome Mr. Wang.
    • Mr. Wang: Hello. Good afternoon. I'm honor to stand here. Hope today's share will be helpful for you.
    • Host: Improving crusher's effect is so important. So can you tell us about the measures to improve double roll crusher effect?
    • Mr. Wang: Yes. I'm glad. Today I will introduce five methods to improve the double roll crusher's effect. First, select the reasonable model of double roll crusher and control the feed diameter. In general, the roller diameter shall be not less 9 times than the clay particle size. Then you can obtain good crushing effect. Second, control the water content of material. The material's moisture content should not be too high. The general should be controlled below 15%, otherwise it will impact crushing efficiency and crushing effect.
    • Host: As far as I know, the double roll crusher has its own crushing process. This process is beneficial to improve its effect, isn't it?
    • Mr. Wang: Yes. When necessary, we can use two sets of double roll crusher for respectively coarse crushing and fine crushing, and fully guarantee the crushing quality. In addition, we should pay attention to the uniform feed. You can't add too many materials. It has better to fall in the roller, in order to reduce the uneven wear of roller.
    • Host: The uniform feed is so vital. Every machine needs to note. I heard that the roller gap can also affect the effect. Is it right?
    • Mr. Wang: You are right. This is the last measures I will say. Two roller gap should be maintained at between 3~5mm. When the clearance is too large, it should be timely repair or replace, otherwise it will impact crushing effect.
    • Host: Oh. This is a meaningful class. Thanks Mr. Wang for your wonderful introduction about the measures to improve double roll crusher effect. Thank you very much. Look forward to your next coming.
    • Mr. Wang: Not at all. Hope to have the opportunity.

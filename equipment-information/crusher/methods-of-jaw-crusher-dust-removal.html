    The Methods Of Jaw Crusher Dust Removal, How To Remove Crusher Dust Methods Of Jaw Crusher Dust Removal

    Description: Environmental protection is the focus of the present era. All walks of life are in promoting conservation and environmental protection. Crushing industry is no exception. In order to reduce the pollution, for the jaw crusher, we must do the dust control measures, and repair dust removal equipment. Only the clean crusher can be developing all the time. Today, we will introduce the several common methods of dust removal. Tell you how to protect environment.

    Inertial Separation Between Dust And Gas

    The inertia of jaw crusher can separate the exceed 4 mm particle and gas in the dust. The gas containing dust can be used for inertial dust separating baffle. Under the impact of baffle, the action of inertia can prevent the effect of scattering, instead of using high speed.

    Electric Dust Collector

    Electric dust collector can make the gas ionization. Gas and solid particle collide. The negative ion and the solid particles are negatively charged. According to the principle, it can achieve the goal. The method is suitable for the fine dust with high requirements, not too high dust concentration, and not too fast gas flow rate.

    Eliminate The Difference Of Height Potential Energy

    The high potential difference of handling equipment, such as hopper, protective cover and chute, are the main reasons to cause the overflow of the dust. Reduce the gap, and reduce the angle of chute. Some closed chambers should be the negative pressure. This is conducive to the dust collection.

    Wet dedusting and exhaust dedusting

    Set spray device in the feeding opening and a belt transfer point. And use water to humidify stone. This can greatly reduce the crushing dust pollution in workshop. Add the centrifugal fan in transshipment point. Strengthen the dust exhausting wind speed and air volume.

    Centrifugal dust removal

    When the gas rotates, it will generate the centrifugal force of hundreds to thousands times than particles gravity. Using its different force, we can achieve the separation goal. Then, we can complete the dust removal task. And we can protect the environment.

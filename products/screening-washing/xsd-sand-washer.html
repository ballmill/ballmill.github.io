  • Name :XSD Sand Washer
  • Description:

    XSD series sand washing machine adopts advanced technology at home and abroad, according to the actual situation of domestic sand industry, to research and develop an efficient machine. It applies to the washing, grading, and impurity removal operations in the metallurgical, building materials, water and electricity industries. For the building sand and sand road, the XSD sand washing machine is more appropriate. Its feed size is 10mm. And its production capacity ranges from 20T/H to 180T/H.

  • Main Structure:

    XSD sand washing machine is made up of the motor, reducer, gear, a feeding tank, washing tank, impeller, washing tank overflow outlet, sand net, a discharge groove, and so on. This important parts are together to finish the whole process of sand washing.

  • Working Principle:

    The motor drives the impeller to rotate slowly through the triangle belt, a speed reducer, and gear. After the material enters into the washing tank, under the action of the impeller, the material grinds with each other. Remove the gravel covered surface impurities, and destruct water vapor coated layer of sand, in order to facilitate the dehydration. At the same time, add water and form strong currents. Timely wash away the impurities, and from the washing tank overflow outlet, then complete cleaning effect. Clean gravel is taken away by the impeller. Finally, the gravel enters into the discharge chute from the rotation of the impeller, and completes the sand cleaning operation.

Working Principle:

1) This crusher uses motor as its power. Through the motor's wheels, the eccentric shaft is driven by the belt and the belt wheel to make the jawstock move by a regulated track.

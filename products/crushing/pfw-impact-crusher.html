    PFW impact crusher is my company's latest product, which is based on PF impact crusher and my company's 20 years experiences. It is mainly used in metallurgy, mining, cement, chemical, refractory materials, ceramic industries, highway construction, water conservancy project and so on.   • Main Structure:

    PFW series jaw crusher is mainly made up of the rotor part, impact support, frame, screw clamshell device (or hydraulic cover open devices) and transmission parts and other components. The wear parts mainly have the rotor, impact plate, hammer and so on. PFW impact crusher has two kinds of cavity type: the two-crusher and the three-crusher. The materials are crushed between the rotor and the impact plate. Through the hydraulic cover open devices, the maintenance personnel can maintain the internal of impact crusher. PFW impact crusher has the outstanding performance advantage, good discharging materials grain sharp and wider scope of application, making users to effectively reduce production and construction cost, operation cost.

Working Principle:

1) This crusher uses motor as its power. Through the motor's wheels, the eccentric shaft is driven by the belt and the belt wheel to make the jawstock move by a regulated track.

2)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become large as the up of the jawstock. This will push the moveable jaw plate towards the fixed jaw plate,which can realize the multi-crushing of the materials.

3)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become small as the down of the jawstock. The moveable jaw plate will leave the jaw plate at the presure of the tension rod and spring ,meanwhile ,the final products will be discharged from the dischargeing opening.

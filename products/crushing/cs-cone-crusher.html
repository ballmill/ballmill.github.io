  • Name :CS Cone Crusher
  • Description:

    CS spring cone crusher is the earliest cone crusher, and it is currently the world's most widely used crushing machinery. CS cone crusher is often used as the secondary crushing or tertiary crushing equipment. It is widely used in metallurgy, building, highway, railway, chemical industry. And it is suitable for crushing hard and medium hard materials, such as iron ore, copper ore, limestone, quartz, granite, diabase, basalt. Its feed size is 13-369mm. The production capacity is 27-1270T/H.

  • Main Structure:

    CS spring cone crusher is composed of six parts: fixed cone assembly, dynamic cone assembly, transmission assembly, spring mechanism, bowl type shaft bracket part, the frame assembly. The auxiliary parts are composed of the electrical system and oil lubrication system. Among them, the fixed cone assembly comprises the feed hopper, adjusting cap, the supporting sleeve, the regulating sleeve, rolling mortar wall, locking screw. The moving cone assembly includes a spindle, dynamic cone body, broken wall, a main shaft sleeve, the cutting ring, a spindle nut, distributing disc. The drive assembly comprises a groove wheel, an expanding sleeve, a transmission shaft, a transmission shaft frame, bevel gear, a big bevel gear, an eccentric sleeve counterweight plate, spindle bushing, rack bushing.

Working Principle:

1) This crusher uses motor as its power. Through the motor's wheels, the eccentric shaft is driven by the belt and the belt wheel to make the jawstock move by a regulated track.

2)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become large as the up of the jawstock. This will push the moveable jaw plate towards the fixed jaw plate,which can realize the multi-crushing of the materials.

3)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become small as the down of the jawstock. The moveable jaw plate will leave the jaw plate at the presure of the tension rod and spring ,meanwhile ,the final products will be discharged from the dischargeing opening.

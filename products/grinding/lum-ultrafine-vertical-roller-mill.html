  • Name :LUM Ultrafine Vertical Roller Mill
  • Description:

    LUM ultrafine vertical roller mill is set ultrafine, grading, and conveying with a suit. It is widely used in chemical industry, metallurgy, non-metallic mineral industries. And it is suitable for grinding the materials that Mohs hardness is below level 5, density is below 3.2, and the compressive strength is below 150Mpa, Such as limestone, calcite, barite, talc, dolomite and other nonmetal ore.   • How to properly operate the vertical roller mill

    1 stable material bed

    The key legislation is stable functioning vertical roller mill material bed, by adjusting the thickness of the material layer height of dam to adjust the thickness of the correspondence between the right and between them and the mill output, the final product will have a direct impact size . Material layer is too thick will affect the grinding efficiency and product size material, the material layer is too thin and can cause excessive grinding machine vibration. The thickness of the material layer can be adjusted according to the wind speed inside the vertical roller mill, the wind speed increase wear, increase the internal circulation of material layer thickness, to reduce wind speed, reducing the inner loop, the material layer thinning.

    2 vertical roller mill milling pressure control

    Pressure grinding mill output will control, power mill grinding efficiency and have a direct impact, the World State LUM series vertical roller mill works by milling process of implementation material layer, grinding pressure depends on the material properties, particle size as well as the volume of feed. Adjusting the fineness of the material on the best way is to find the right time trial production of grinding pressure and wind pressure can form a reasonable good internal circulation, to ensure stable and efficient milling efficiency.

    3 to guarantee a certain temperature the vertical roller mill

    ZENITH LUM series vertical roller mill is a set of crushing, grinding, grading, milling and drying in one system, the mill drying air temperature is a measure of whether the normal operation of the integrated indicators. The material currently on the market size and the drying effect is very high, in order to ensure that the finished material is less than 0.5% moisture content, the general control of the vertical roller mill outlet temperature of about 90 ~ C. The grinding of the air temperature is too high will affect cleaning effect, the air temperature is too low will affect the mill separator efficiency, air temperature and wind speed to do for a reasonable adjustment.

Working Principle:

1) This crusher uses motor as its power. Through the motor's wheels, the eccentric shaft is driven by the belt and the belt wheel to make the jawstock move by a regulated track.

2)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become large as the up of the jawstock. This will push the moveable jaw plate towards the fixed jaw plate,which can realize the multi-crushing of the materials.

3)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become small as the down of the jawstock. The moveable jaw plate will leave the jaw plate at the presure of the tension rod and spring ,meanwhile ,the final products will be discharged from the dischargeing opening.

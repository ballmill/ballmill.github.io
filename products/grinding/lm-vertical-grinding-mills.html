  • Name :LM Vertical Grinding Mills
  • Description:

    LM vertical milling machine is developed by using the advanced technology at home and abroad. It sets crushing, drying, grinding, classification and conveying with a suit, and is the ideal grinding product. It is widely used in cement, metallurgy, chemical industry, hydropower, mineral and other industries. And it is suitable for grinding of cement, coal, feldspar, calcite, fluorite, talc, iron ore, etc. The maximum feed particle size is 38~55mm. And the production capacity is 10~50T/H.

  • Main Structure:

    LM vertical grinding mill mainly consists of the classifier, the transmission arm, grinding plate, grinding roller, hydraulic device, pressure device, reducer, feed opening, limiting device, and so on. The grinding roller is the main part to grind the material. The pressure device brings press for the grinding roller to grind material.

Working Principle:

1) This crusher uses motor as its power. Through the motor's wheels, the eccentric shaft is driven by the belt and the belt wheel to make the jawstock move by a regulated track.

2)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become large as the up of the jawstock. This will push the moveable jaw plate towards the fixed jaw plate,which can realize the multi-crushing of the materials.

3)The angle between the toggie plate and jawstock will become small as the down of the jawstock. The moveable jaw plate will leave the jaw plate at the presure of the tension rod and spring ,meanwhile ,the final products will be discharged from the dischargeing opening.
